About the club

We are a mostly vanilla survival Minecraft server

Our goal is to work together and have fun. We are looking to have a small group of interesting players build with us.

We are a free server and will never have ads unless we can make loads of cash from it. Who knows, you could get a cut. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
